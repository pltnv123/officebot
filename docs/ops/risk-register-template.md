# Risk Register Template

## Risk ID
Unique label for reference.

## Description
Describe the risk including triggers and context.

## Impact
Explain what breaks if the risk materializes.

## Likelihood
Estimate probability (low/medium/high).

## Mitigation
Actions to lower likelihood or impact.

## Owner
Person or role responsible for monitoring.

## Status
Current state (e.g., Active, Mitigated, Closed).
